WHO ARE MEDICAL TECHNOLOGISTS?

They are referred as clinical laboratory scientists. Medical Technologist is a health care professional who performs diagnostic analytical tests and maintained quality assurance. He works in clinical laboratory at hospital, research laboratories, reference laboratories and within the biotechnology industry.

There is very important and prominent role of Medical Lab. Technologist in health sector as they have associate degree in the field of health diagnosis and to provide basic and up to date facilities of diagnosis to the people of nation.

About Us

Medical Lab of Pakistan (MLP) is an independent, non-governmental and non-profit organization working for the rights and registration of Medical Technologists/Medical Laboratory Professionals and the Students of Allied Health Sciences in Pakistan. Medilab is also involved in different academic and awareness activities by arranging annual Seminars, Workshops, Lectures and Hands on Training for the Medical Lab. Professionals.
